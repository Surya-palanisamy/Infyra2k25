Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Mission

To foster collaboration among students, researchers, and industry professionals through innovative competitions, hands-on workshops, and inspiring lectures that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications.

🚀

Vision

To establish INFYRA as India's premier technology festival, driving innovation and creativity while nurturing the next generation of tech leaders and entrepreneurs who will shape the future of technology.

Department of Information Technology

Established in 2008, the Department of Information Technology stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education and research. With a strong foundation built over more than a decade, our department has consistently delivered outstanding results in academics, research, and industry collaboration.
