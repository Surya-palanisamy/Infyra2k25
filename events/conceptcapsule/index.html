Event Concept

Concept Capsule is a high-energy idea pitching challenge where participants don’t come prepared—they draw their problem statement on the spot and must build an innovative solution using a provided template. Time pressure will push their creativity, logic, and pitch skills to the limit!

Format & Instructions

Teams will draw a problem capsule, receive a solution template, and get time to build an MVP + pitch.

Team Formation

Participants will compete in teams of 2–4 members.

Pitch

Each team presents their innovative solution to judges and audience within a strict time window.

Rules

Problem draw
Teams draw a sealed capsule to reveal the prompt.
Development
Teams get a fixed time to craft an MVP on the provided template.
Pitch
Each team presents their pitch + Q&A.
