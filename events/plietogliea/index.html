Event Concept

Each team selects one member to be Gaia (Earth Goddess). Others plead for salvation from an environmental disaster, crafting socially-aware pleas to convince Gaia to save them and the planet.

Format & Instructions

Preparation

10 minutes to prepare a 3–5 minute plea. Gaia evaluates critically.

Team Formation

Teams of 4. One member plays Gaia, others plead as humans.

Judging

Creativity, social awareness, emotional impact and teamwork.

Rules

  1. 3–5 minutes per plea, 10 minutes prep.
  2. Original content only.
  3. Respectful language.
  4. Gaia's decision is final.
  5. Teams must register before the event.
