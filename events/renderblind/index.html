RENDER BLIND

Event Concept

RenderBlind is a two-round design challenge that tests both creativity and execution under unique constraints.

Round 1 (Online): Participants will design based on a single given theme. From this, 7 teams will be shortlisted.
Round 2 (Offline): The shortlisted teams will compete in the final round, which includes an additional challenge conducted offline.

Format & Instructions

  • Team Formation: Teams of 2 participants only.
  • Teams must bring their own laptops.
  • Strictly no AI tools or external help.
  • Only Figma/Canva allowed.
